About TrueNorth

TrueNorth is a smartphone-based app that provides real-time, audio and haptic indoor navigation for blind and visually impaired users. Using advanced computer vision, it helps you move confidently through complex indoor spaces like malls, hospitals, and metros—no extra hardware or building modifications needed.

Key Features

  • ✅ Turn-by-turn audio guidance
  • ✅ Real-time obstacle detection
  • ✅ Smartwatch haptic support
  • ✅ Works in any mapped building
  • ✅ Voice command enabled
  • ✅ Private & hands-free

How It Works

  1. Download the app
  2. Scan your surroundings with your phone
  3. Select your destination using voice
  4. Receive instant guidance and obstacle alerts
